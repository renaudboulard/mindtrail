# Marketing Strategy for MindTrail

## 1. Content Marketing & SEO (High Priority, Low Cost)

### Blog / Articles (add to Astro site)

- "Why 67% of Ultra DNFs Are Mental — And What to Do About It"
- "PETTLEP Visualization for Runners: A Complete Guide"
- "Mental Training Plan for UTMB / [Major Race]" (target race-specific long-tail keywords)
- "Night Running Anxiety: How Visualization Helps"
- "First Ultra? Here's What Nobody Tells You About the Mental Game"

These target runners Googling race prep, DNF prevention, and mental training — the exact buyer profile.

### SEO Quick Wins (currently missing)

- Add JSON-LD structured data (Product schema, FAQ schema)
- Add Google Analytics / Plausible for tracking
- Create a `sitemap.xml` and `robots.txt`
- Target keywords: "mental training ultra running", "visualization for trail runners", "PETTLEP running"

---

## 2. Community-First Distribution (Highest ROI)

### Reddit — r/ultrarunning, r/trailrunning, r/running (120K+ combined)

- Share value-first posts about mental training science (not product links)
- Answer DNF/mental struggle threads with genuine advice, link in profile
- AMA: "I built an audio mental training program for ultra runners based on sport psychology research"

### Strava Clubs & Facebook Groups

- Join ultra-specific groups (UTMB Community, Ultra Running Community, etc.)
- Share the University of Plymouth study as a conversation starter
- Offer free Session 1 preview to group members

### Trail Running Forums (iRunFar, LetsRun ultra section)

- Contribute genuinely, become known for mental training expertise

---

## 3. Podcast Strategy (Best Channel for Audio Products)

### Guest Appearances (pitch to)

- *Trail Runner Nation* — largest trail running podcast
- *Ultra Runner Podcast* / *CCC Ultra Podcast*
- *Science of Ultra* — perfect fit for the research-backed approach
- *The Running Public* — ultra-focused content
- *Rich Roll Podcast* — broader endurance audience

### Pitch angle

"University of Plymouth found non-runners trained with FIT were 5x more likely to finish an ultra — I turned that research into a practical audio program"

---

## 4. Race-Event Marketing (Seasonal, High Intent)

### Race Calendar Targeting

- UTMB (Aug) — start content push 8-10 weeks before
- Western States (Jun), Hardrock, MIUT, Lavaredo
- Create race-specific landing pages: "Mental Training for UTMB 2026"

### Race Expos & Partnerships

- Partner with race organizers for expo booths or digital goodie bags
- Offer 10% discount codes through race newsletters
- Sponsor aid stations (brand visibility with exact target audience)

---

## 5. Influencer & Ambassador Program

### Micro-influencers (1K-20K followers) in trail running

- Gift free access in exchange for honest review
- Target athletes who've spoken openly about mental struggles in races
- Instagram/YouTube trail running creators

### Coaches & Training Plans

- Partner with ultra running coaches (Jason Koop, David Roche, etc.)
- Offer affiliate commission (Lemon Squeezy supports this natively)
- Position MindTrail as a complement to physical training plans

---

## 6. Email Marketing (Conversion Engine)

### Lead Magnet: Free Session 1

"Get Your Free Mental Training Session for Trail Runners" in exchange for email.

### 5-email nurture sequence

1. Welcome + Session 1 access
2. The science behind visualization (Plymouth study)
3. Testimonial spotlight + specific scenario (night running)
4. "What's in the full program" breakdown
5. Limited-time offer or social proof push

### Tools

ConvertKit or Mailchimp, integrate with Lemon Squeezy.

---

## 7. Paid Ads (After Organic Traction)

### Start small with

- **Instagram/Facebook Ads** targeting: trail running, ultra running, UTMB, Salomon, Hoka interests
- **Google Ads** on high-intent keywords: "mental training ultramarathon", "visualization for runners"
- Budget: $10-20/day to test, scale what converts

### Ad creative angles

- "67% of ultra DNFs are mental. Train the other 67%."
- "Run your race in your mind before you run it with your legs"
- Short audio clip teaser from Session 1

## 8. Strategic Partnerships

- **Salomon / Hoka / COROS** — approach for co-marketing (mental training content series)

## Key Insight

The strongest lever is **content + community**. Ultra runners are a tight-knit, research-curious community. Leading with the science (Plymouth study, PETTLEP framework) and genuine value in running forums/podcasts will build trust faster than any ad spend. The free Session 1 as a lead magnet is the best conversion tool — let the product sell itself.
